Our Itinerary

08/06 - Palermo/Milazzo

After a long day travelling, it's time to enjoy whatever we can in Palermo before taking the train to Milazzo, where we'll finish the day with a lovely italian beer!

09/06 - Cefalu

It's beach time! Let's get the train to Cefalu and take the whole day off underneath the sun

10/06 - Taormina

Another day in the beach, this time: Taormina! Let's get to know another beautiful spot in Sicily!

11/06 - Milazzo

Starting the day with a visit to the castle, then a walk in the centre, some time on the lovely Milazzo's beach and finishing the day at the best party ever! #HorizonSunset

12/06 - Catania

Have you got sick of beaches? No? Ok, in Catania we'll have more beaches to explore! Yes? So don't worry at all, we also have good options for the nightime or to do shopping.

13/06 - Vulcano

Have you been at a Vulcano before? Nope? So now it's the time to do it! We'll enjoy the black sand beaches, get to know the spa and maybe meet the Vulcano - a whole joyful day before leaving Sicily.

Extra tour - Etna

Speaking of vulcanos, why don't go to another one in the middle of Sicily and explore the beauties of the montains?

Extra tour - Portorosa

Why leave Sicily before saying goodbye to one of the best places in Sicily? Let's visit Portorosa and thank again for the best experiences of my life and the intense personal development I've had since then.

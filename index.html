Our Itinerary

08/06 - Palermo/Milazzo

After a long day travelling, it's time to enjoy whatever we can in Palermo before taking the train to Milazzo, where we'll finish the day with a lovely italian beer!

09/06 - Siracusa

It's boat time! Let's get the train to Siracusa and take the whole day off underneath the sun - including a 4h tour in a boat all-inclusive!
Also, let's know the lovely castle of Ortigia.

10/06 - Etna/Alcantara Park

Now it's time to enjoy the view of the vulcano and get to know the beautiful Natural Reserve of Alcantara Park - where the warm lava crosses the cold water and create an awesome view!

11/06 - Portorosa/Milazzo

Sunday's the day to know the paradise known as Portorosa! A beautiful beach with the view of the Eolian Islands and transparent waters. Let's relax and enjoy the sun, the waves sound and good company!

12/06 - Isole Eoli

Have you sick of beaches? Hope not, because we'll take another boat trip to the Eoli islands of Vulcano and Lipari! Two wonderful islands including another castle, mud baths and black sand beaches! Amazing, isn't?

13/06 - Milazzo/Palermo

It's time to say goodbye, but we still have time to visit the best view of Milazzo, from the Castle of Milazzo!
